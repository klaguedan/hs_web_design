My Autobiography
Current

Right now, I'm pretty content with where I am with life. I'm told that I'm very responsible and rational for my age. I'm also called smart a lot, but I prefer to keep humble about my positive qualities.

Switching to CAVA marks the second time that I've moved schools. It's my first semester here and so far I'm loving it!

It's hard to shake the habit of wearing all-black since it's such a flattering color, but I've been expanding my wardrobe to other shades. My friends are proud to know that I now include the color pink often. Fun fact! Despite many pieces of evidence that my favorite color should be black, it's actually pink!

I still study daily on Khan Academy and in my opinion, it's what I owe all my math skills to. Thank goodness they've expanded their libraries since middle school! Although now, I have to study for the SATs.

A positive change is that I routinely hang out with friends and family over the weekend. I'll admit that it's quite nice to walk around cities once in a while. Myself from 3 years ago would've never accepted the opportunity to go on exciting adventures.

I'm posing for a group photo with my friends. My eyes are unfortunately closed, but at least there's a cute dog.
