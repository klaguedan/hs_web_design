My Autobiography
Infant

I was born on September 21, 2002 in the Philippines. Other than my mother, I only had an older sister and at this point, we still lived with my cousins, aunts, and uncles.

I only have the memories of my family members to trust since I don't remember much about my behavior as an infant but apparently, I was very energetic and always played with toys.

Of course, I grew up with English as my second language, Ilocano being my first. However now, I can't really communicate fluently in my mother language.

In my old town I would always run around outside in the dirt with neighborhood kids. I would draw in the dirt with sticks and occasionally, I'd say hi to people passing by our house.

Soon though, I'd have to leave.

A photo of me as a baby in the Philippines. It seems like I'm holding a basketball. Too bad I don't play any sports right now.
