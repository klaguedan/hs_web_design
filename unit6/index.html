My Autobiography
Home

My name is Katrina and I'm my mother's youngest child. However, I am certainly NOT the youngest person in the family.

I'm known as the family's nerd because I always study topics that I want to learn more about. I'm very easygoing and kind, but I am also very loud with people that I'm comfortable with!

Besides liking computers and math, I love to create art whether it's visual or auditory. I doodle while I do work and draw bigger pieces when I have free time. Additionally, my family has a long line of good singers, and I'm proud to continue the legacy.

Luckily, I've only lived for sixteen years so there's only small amount of embarassing moments for you to read through.

Actually, I'm not drinking tea. It's warm milk. But as my generation says it, "I'll spill the tea" about my life.

(Tea = drama, interesting events, or information)
