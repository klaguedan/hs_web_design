Guitar Progress

My Materials

  • Electric guitar: mine happens to be a black and peach fender.
  • Amp: this lets me play with more volume or with additional effects.
  • Pick: I normally pluck with my fingers, but I play some songs with picks too.

A Very Limited Repertoire

Since I only casually play guitar, there's not many full songs that I know how to play. In general, I'm more inclined to pick up a new song if it has very pretty melodies that are playable in fingerstyle.

Regardless, here are some songs I'm making progress on:

  • "Voice of No Return" from Nier: Automata, which is a video game.
  • "A Town by the Sea" from Kiki's Delivery Service, a movie.
  • "Adventure Sprit" by Yvette Young. She plays a genre called math rock.
  • "A Part of Me" by Neck Deep. They're a punk band, but this song is acoustic.
  • "Night Owls" by Mree. A very ethereal song with lyrics too.
  • "Gymnopedie 1" by Erik Satie. The simple melody is very pleasing to hear.
