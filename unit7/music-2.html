Current Music

My Materials

  • Headphones: listening to music on my own feels more comfortable that listening out loud. I can hear small details more easily.
  • Computer or phone: I prefer listening to music digitally.

Spotify

Most of my time spent listening to music and most of the new artists or songs I discover are on Spotify. There are many convenient features that make listening to music more fun.

When I don't feel like playing one of my playlists, I just look browse the premade genre categories from Spotify. I also am subscribed to a weekly customized playlist of recommended songs based on my listener profile.

My Music Taste Explained

Another cool feature Spotify has is that I'm able to look at statistics of my listening such as average beats per minute, most active time, and top genres.

Although the data above quite clearly analyzes what genre I listen to the most, the data is still restricted by time. My taste is always changing and it just so happens that when I captured those results, Australian Indie, Pop, Indietronica, Pop Punk, Screamo, and Electropop were the top 6 genres at that moment.

My ears are biased towards melodic electronic music and jazz. I quite like bands too.

Music Phases

My music taste now is the accumulation of all the music phases that I've been through, that is, the stages in which I was fixated with a particular genre.

From what I can remember, and as I am currently in 11th grade, these have been my music phases:

  • Electronic (6th grade)
  • Post-Hardcore Rock (7th grade)
  • J-Pop (7th grade)
  • Indie (8th grade)
