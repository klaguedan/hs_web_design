Taking Notes

The act of writing down condensed information focusing on a specific concept.

How did I start?

Whenever I learn something new, I'm always excited by the prospect of writing notes on it. In 9th grade, I saw posts of very organized, decorated studying notes and I was inspired to start my own set of pretty notes too. I didn't need to shop around for this hobby since I already had the essentials: paper and something to write with. Plus, writing notes would be an excuse to practice my handwriting and calligraphy.

Notes for which topics?

  • Math
  • Music
  • World Languages
  • Web Design
  • Video Games
  • Songs

I take notes spontaneously on whatever I feel like I want to remember.
